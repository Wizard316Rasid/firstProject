Instruction guide

I know the video doesn't explain it well, but first you start with a sideways gainer, putting one foot on the mattres depending on if youre a righty or a lefty. If you are a lefty you will aproach the mattres from the left, and jump with youre left foot throwing yourself backwards or over the shoulder and landing on your stomach, for righties it is the same thing but on your right. When you feel comfortable you can start jumping more over your back, until you get to fully going on your back.

When you feel comfortable, and this is very important not to rush because this is a very dangerous move, you will start jumping whith both legs on to the mattres, and if you need to you can wait a few seconds to then jump over youre side until you get brave enough to stop jumping over youre side and jump backwards. Later you will start not having a run up and start jumping just of the mattres, if you want yo can jump sideways at first and then start jumping over you back onto youre stomach. After a while you can start jumping on to youre knees and than on youre feet.
